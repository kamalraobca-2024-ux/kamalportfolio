About Me

The person behind the pixels

I'm Kamal Rao, a creative designer and video editor who blends storytelling with technology. Over the past few years I've helped brands turn raw footage and rough ideas into polished reels, AI-assisted advertisements, and marketing campaigns that actually move people to act.

My work sits at the intersection of design, motion and AI — from cutting cinematic edits in Premiere Pro and After Effects, to generating AI-driven visuals, to building the graphics and copy that carry a brand's voice across social platforms.
